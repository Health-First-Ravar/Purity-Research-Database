## Visual Identity Philosophy — "Nature Perfected"

Just as our coffee is "nature perfected through science," our visual identity is "nature's
beauty refined through artistic mastery." Our imagery must be as revolutionary as our
product. Every visual expression, from photography to video to web design, must match the
excellence of what's in the cup. This isn't an aspiration, it's an obligation. When you've
created the world's healthiest coffee through an uncompromising commitment to excellence,
anything less than a world-class visual identity is unacceptable.

**If it's not the best in the coffee industry in every way, it's not Purity.**

## Logo Guidelines

The Purity Coffee logo is designed and inspired by the Fibonacci sequence, known to be seen
across nature. The logo is well-balanced, centered and bold. It is the primary expression
of flow. Teal and black are the default expression.

- **Logo Mark (the "P")** — secondary shorthand for limited space (social avatar, app
  icon, favicon) or places where the primary mark has already been established.
- **Variations** — horizontal stacked and horizontal wordmark, available for unique cases
  where the primary logo doesn't fit.
- **Exclusion Zone** — always keep the "P" from Purity as the minimum clear space around
  the logo mark.
- **Black & White** — used when color is not possible.
- **Don'ts** — never manipulate the "P" (flip, rotate). Never use colors other than black
  or white for the Purity wordmark. Never use colors other than black, white, or brand
  aqua for the logo mark. Don't use the "P" logo to manipulate the wordmark.
- **Application over imagery** — always optimize for logo clarity. Find a quiet space
  within the image with strong contrast. Don't cover key elements.

## Typography

Two fonts for all communication, each with an intended use.

| Role | Font | Usage |
|------|------|-------|
| Headline | Nominee Bold | Primary headlines |
| Subhead | Nominee Regular | Subheads |
| Body Copy | Nominee Regular | Paragraph text |
| Special Callouts | Franziska Italic / Regular | Pull quotes, accent type |

Hierarchy is essential. Create contrast between scale, weight, and font to achieve focus.

## Photography Direction

In accordance with our Brand Filter, Purity photographs are vibrant, radiant, sharp,
saturated, color-forward and always beautiful. Never dull, washed out, grainy, faded,
fuzzy, muted or blurry.

- **Product Photography** — hero shots, celebrity portraits. Coffee bags set in nature like
  jewels in a jewel box. High design without being surreal.
- **Coffee Photography** — all forms: grown, harvested, processed, roasted, packaged,
  prepared. Include action (pouring, drinking, harvesting, roasting) whenever possible.
- **Origin Photography** — real people (farmers), real places (plantations and farms),
  lower thirds whenever possible. **All farm footage comes exclusively from Purity's own
  coffee farms**, highlighting stewardship, care, and human partnership with living systems.
- **Lifestyle Photography** — a body in balance, living within nature. Subjects appear
  healthy, grounded, at ease, in natural environments that reinforce wellbeing.

## Video Direction

- **Logo placement** — full white logo as intro or endcards. "P" unravels from top to
  bottom in animations.
- **Typography in video** — Nominee Bold for headlines/accentuated statements (200pt).
  Franziska OT 60pt in yellow or white with dark drop shadow for speaking captions.
- **Color grading** — light and vibrant but not over-saturated.
- **Pace and transitions** — coffee-related shots drive forward (never fast, never
  lingering). Nature, origin, and roasting shots can breathe — should feel like a painting.
  Sit on one scene while action happens within the still frame (leaves blowing, farmers
  picking cherries, beans spinning in a drying pan).
- **Visual style** — minimal with a single central action. Coffee always in motion. Always
  an element of nature juxtaposed with the coffee. Every shot rooted in reality. More
  National Geographic than documentary. Minimize talking heads.
- **Voiceover** — authoritative and knowledgeable; approachable and earnest; clear and
  comprehensive; engaging, never boring.
- **Animation** — precise and intentional. Every animated element serves the larger vision.
  The "P" in the Purity logo unravels.

## Graphics & Application

- **Promotional Icons & Badges** — for digital marketing campaigns. Use existing assets as
  reference to create new ones.
- **Informational Icons** — clear messaging about products, education, trust, health
  benefits. Categories: Origin, Science, Roasting.
- **Color application** — all graphics follow the brand color system, and must align with
  the Brand Filter (Purity, Vitality, Authority).

## Campaign Examples

### Campaign 1 — "Redefining Great Coffee"

Wide shot of coffee being poured into a cup in slow motion. Camera slowly zooms in.
Voiceover: "What is truly great coffee? Is it organic certified? Regeneratively grown? Is
it mold and mycotoxin free? Is it loaded with antioxidants and nutrients that protect your
health? Or does it just taste really good? Well maybe...just maybe...it's all of the
above." Snap to someone taking a sip and placing the cup next to a bag of Purity. "Purity
Coffee. Redefining Great Coffee."

### Campaign 2 — "Nurtured by Nature. Perfected by Science."

Photograph of a bag of Purity Coffee next to a full mug of coffee at the farm where the
beans were grown. "Nurtured by Nature. Perfected by Science. Healthy for You."

### Campaign 3 — "Crafted for Health"

Voiceover over footage showing Purity's progression from seed to cup: "Crafted for
fostering the natural environment. Crafted for high antioxidants and nutrients. Crafted
for clean and delicious sips. Crafted for easy digestion and long-term wellness. Crafted
for a new idea of what coffee can be. Purity Coffee. Crafted for Health."
