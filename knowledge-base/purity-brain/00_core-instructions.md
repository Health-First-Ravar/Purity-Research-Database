## Purity Coffee Goal-Setting Framework

Structure aligning long-term mission to quarterly execution:

- **Shared Mission** — enduring north star; unmeasurable but orients all work
- **Company-Wide Goals** — multi-year strategic priorities, reaffirmed annually
- **Annual Company-Wide OKRs** — this year's success definition for active goals; single
  leadership owner per objective, measurable Key Results owned by others
- **Quarterly Departmental OKRs** — 3–5 per department supporting company goals or
  department-critical work; owned by department heads
- **Quarterly Team OKRs** — cross-functional / project-based (NPD, Sustainability, Coffee
  book, Crowdfunding); owned by team leads
- **Quarterly Individual OKRs** — personal priorities mapping to department/team OKRs or
  the shared mission
- **Rocks** — binary quarterly commitments; each owned by one person; done or not done

### Meeting cadence aligned to goal-setting philosophy

1. **Weekly Leadership Team Meeting** — Tuesdays, up to 1 hr
2. **Weekly/Bi-weekly CEO Check-In** — Thursdays as needed, up to 30 min
3. **Annual In-Person Leadership Planning** — mid-Q4, multi-day
4. **Quarterly Departmental OKR Planning** — 1–2 weeks before quarter end, 2–3 hrs
5. **Monthly OKR Progress Review** — last week of each month, 1 hr
6. **Monthly Company-Wide Update** — second Wednesday of each month, 1 hr
7. **Weekly or ad-hoc department/team syncs**

### Key principle

"Company-wide OKRs are not a reflection of personal value or initiative importance —
they're the shared bets we're doubling down on this year."

"Rocks are the tangible 90-day promises we make to move OKRs forward."
