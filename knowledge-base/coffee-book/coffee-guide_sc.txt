               What if there was a simple daily ritual that could lower your risk of liver
             disease, type 2 diabetes, and certain cancers, while helping you live longer
                   and fight inflammation? The right cup of coffee could do this.
The Coffee Guide to Better Health by Ildi Revi, Chief Learning Officer at Purity Coffee, shows how coffee, when grown, processed,
roasted, and brewed with health as the guiding priority, becomes a powerful tool for both personal and planetary wellness. Built
around the concept of Circular Health Coffee, this science-based guide unites coffee chemistry, organic regenerative farming,
food safety, and functional health into one clear, actionable framework.

Inside, you will learn:

     • Why coffee is much more than caffeine, and how compounds such as chlorogenic acids and other antioxidants
       contribute to its health benefits
     • How producers and roasters make intentional choices to preserve and enhance coffee’s wellness properties
     • What health-grade green coffee standards are and why the industry urgently needs them
     • How to evaluate decaffeination methods and select the healthiest options
     • What lab testing reveals and how transparency empowers health-aligned choices
     • Practical guidance for sourcing, brewing, and enjoying coffee as a functional food, with full-color visuals that make
       complex science understandable

Written primarily for wellness professionals, including physicians, nutritionists, and health coaches, and for coffee professionals,
including farmers, roasters, and importers, this book also serves highly informed consumers who want to explore coffee’s full
potential through research, standards, and practice.

Drawing on decades of hands-on experience, Revi offers a rigorous yet practical approach for those who want to reduce disease
risk, improve well-being, and reimagine coffee through a health-first lens.

Circular Health Coffee is more than a trend, it is a blueprint for coffee’s future.

Read it. Brew it. Live it.

Ildi Revi, M.Ad.Ed., CPT, PCQI, former Q Instructor, brings nearly thirty years of experience at the intersection of education,
agriculture, and coffee.

Her career began in adult education in Chicago and continued in Mozambique, where she developed university training
programs, founded a national English language theatre competition, and trained teachers and Peace Corps volunteers. She
later spent almost a decade on a coffee farm in Zimbabwe’s Vumba Mountains, where she created farmer education programs
for World Vision and USAID while founding Leopard Forest Coffee Company, her roastery in South Carolina.

Ildi went on to design global certificate programs for the Specialty Coffee Association and later directed performance and
learning systems at Ally Coffee. Today at Purity Coffee she leads research on coffee, health, and sustainability, traveling
worldwide to collaborate with farmers, scientists, and industry partners.
